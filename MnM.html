M&M Cookies

Biscuit

Ingredients:

2 Cups Vanilla Ice Cream 2/3 Cup Milk 8 Oreo Cookies 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract (Optional) Whipped Cream for serving (Optional)

Instructions:

Add the ice cream, milk and Oreo cookies to your blender. Pulse until mostly smooth. Serve in large glasses with whipped cream if desired.
